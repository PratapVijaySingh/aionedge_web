Our Mission

Democratizing AI for Enterprise Process Optimization

Our Vision

Building the Future of Intelligent Business Operations

As a startup, we envision becoming the go-to partner for businesses seeking innovative AI solutions. We see a future where custom RAG agents and intelligent automation are accessible to every organization, regardless of size. Our vision is to create a world where AI-powered process optimization is not just a luxury for large enterprises, but a standard tool for smart businesses.

By 2027, we aim to be the leading startup in custom AI agent development, with a strong presence in the Dutch legal market and expanding across Europe. We're building the foundation for a company that will revolutionize how businesses approach process optimization, starting with our innovative RAG technology and expanding into comprehensive AI solutions.
